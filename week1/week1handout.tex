\section{Getting the most (grades) out of this
class}\label{getting-the-most-grades-out-of-this-class}

\begin{itemize}
\item
  Podcast and Article Responses (5\%) {[}one two-page doc every other
  week{]}
\item
  Problem Sets (20\%) {[}seven to eight in total{]}
\item
  Mid-term exam (20\%) {[}in-class, written, closed book{]}
\item
  Final exam (20\%) {[}in-class, written, closed book{]}
\item
  \emph{Final project} (25\%)

  \begin{quote}
  The project must include: (1) introduction to your question and data,
  (2) data visualization addressing your question, (3) regression
  analysis with plain-English interpretation, (4) limitations and
  potential improvements, and (5) link to GitHub repository with all
  code.
  \end{quote}
\end{itemize}
